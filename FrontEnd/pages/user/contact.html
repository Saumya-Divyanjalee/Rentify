FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

How do I book a cab with Rentify?
+
You can book via our website, WhatsApp (+94 77 123 4567), phone call, or walk into our Colombo office. Instant booking confirmation is sent within minutes.
What vehicle types do you offer?
+
We offer Cars (Standard & Luxury), Vans, Minibuses, Coaches, Wheelchair-accessible vehicles, and Cab/Taxi services across all Sri Lankan cities.
Can I cancel or reschedule my booking?
+
Yes! Free cancellation is available up to 1 hour before your scheduled pickup. Rescheduling can be done any time without extra charge.
Do you cover island-wide destinations?
+
Absolutely. We cover 48+ cities across Sri Lanka — from Jaffna in the north to Matara in the south, and everywhere in between.
Are your drivers licensed and verified?
+
Every Rentify driver undergoes background checks, defensive driving training, and language assessments before joining our fleet. All carry valid licenses.
Do you offer airport transfer services?
+
Yes! We provide meet-and-greet airport transfers from Bandaranaike International Airport with flight tracking, fixed rates, and name-board pickup service.
How are prices calculated?
+
Most of our services are priced per KM with a minimum charge. We also offer fixed-rate packages for popular routes. All prices are shown upfront — no hidden fees.
Can I book for weddings and events?
+
Yes! We offer decorated premium vehicles for weddings, engagement parties, corporate events and VIP occasions. Contact us for a custom quote and availability.
