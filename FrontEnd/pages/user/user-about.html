Our Story

Built Around Your
Journey

Rentify was founded in 2018 with a simple mission: to make every journey across Sri Lanka safe, affordable, and enjoyable. Starting with just 5 cabs in Colombo, we've grown into a nationwide travel service trusted by thousands.

Whether you're heading to the misty highlands of Nuwara Eliya, the golden beaches of Mirissa, or navigating the city rush — Rentify gets you there with professionalism and care.
